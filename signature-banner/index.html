Step 3 · Desktop

Add below your existing signature (desktop)

Gmail

Settings → See all settings → General → Signature → click at the end of your existing signature → press Enter once → paste with Cmd+V (Mac) or Ctrl+V (Windows) → Save Changes.

Outlook (web)

Settings → View all Outlook settings → Mail → Compose and reply → click at the end of your existing signature → press Enter → paste with Cmd+V / Ctrl+V → Save.

Outlook (Mac desktop)

Outlook → Preferences → Signatures → select your signature → click at the end of the text → paste with Cmd+V.

Outlook (Windows desktop)

File → Options → Mail → Signatures → select your signature → click at the end of the text → paste with Ctrl+V → OK.
Step 3 · Mobile

Add below your existing signature (phone)

Open this builder on your phone (Safari or Chrome), tap Copy Mobile Banner above, then follow the steps for your app.

Apple Mail (iPhone / iPad)

Settings app → Apps → Mail → Signature → choose Per Account → tap inside your signature → place the cursor at the end → tap to bring up the menu → Paste → shake the phone → Undo Change Attributes (this preserves the banner formatting).

Gmail app (iPhone / Android)

Open Gmail → tap your profile picture → Settings → tap your account → Mobile Signature → tap inside the field → place the cursor at the end → tap and hold → Paste → OK.
Note: Gmail mobile signatures are plain text. The banner will paste as a link; for full formatting, the signature set on Gmail web (desktop) is used when sending from any device.

Outlook app (iPhone / Android)

Open Outlook → tap your profile (top-left) → Settings (gear icon) → Signature → tap inside the signature field → place the cursor at the end → tap and hold → Paste → checkmark to save.

Inside a single email (any app)

If you don’t want to change your default signature, paste the mobile banner into the body of an email right above where you’d normally type: tap and hold at the end of your signature → Paste.
