About PAWSIC

Uniting the Post-Acute
Wound Care Community

We are a patient-centered, interprofessional council providing evidence-based education, clinical tools, and a professional community for everyone who cares about wound and skin health outside the hospital.

Mission & Vision What We Do Who We Serve Leadership
Our Story

How PAWSIC Began, and Where We're Heading

Our Mission

We are a patient-centered inter-professional team that serves the post-acute care community as the source and voice to guide in quality wound and skin prevention and management, through collaborative evidence-based education, practice, and advocacy.

PAWSIC is a 501(c)(6) nonprofit council led by nationally recognized wound care experts.

Become a Member →

Our Vision

To create a future where post-acute wound management is patient-centered, addressing not only the wound and skin but also the person's unique needs, preferences, and overall well-being.

What We Do

How PAWSIC Serves the Community

Through education, advocacy, and community building, we work to raise the standard of wound and skin care for every patient in post-acute settings.

Evidence-Based Education

Accredited CE/CME webinars, courses, and clinical resources grounded in current peer-reviewed evidence, for every level of the care team.

Interprofessional Community

Forums, shared interest groups (SIGs), and working groups that connect wound care clinicians, facility leaders, industry partners, and caregivers nationwide.

Advocacy & Standards

Promoting elevated wound care standards in skilled nursing, long-term care, home health, and rehab settings, giving clinicians a voice in policy conversations.

Events & Conferences

Annual conferences, workshops, and sponsored webinars that bring the wound care community together to learn, connect, and advance practice.

Sponsorship & Partnerships

Connecting sponsors and industry partners with an engaged, multidisciplinary wound care audience while maintaining strict educational independence.

Membership & Resources

Free and premium membership tiers with access to clinical tools, checklists, publications, webinar libraries, and the PAWSIC Skin Failure Library.

Who We Serve

Built for the Full Care Team

PAWSIC is for everyone who touches wound and skin care outside the emergency, inpatient, or surgical setting. There is no credential requirement to join. Just a commitment to better patient outcomes.

Patients & Their Families

Patients navigating wound and skin care, and the families who support them.

Caregivers & CNAs

Frontline caregivers and certified nursing assistants providing hands-on wound and skin care.

Nurses & Wound Clinicians

RNs, LPNs, and wound care specialists across all post-acute settings.

Physicians, NPs, PAs & DPMs

Medical directors, physicians, nurse practitioners, physician assistants, and podiatrists.

Therapists (PT, OT, SLP)

Physical, occupational, and speech-language therapists involved in wound and skin care.

Pharmacists, Dietitians & Specialists

Allied health professionals supporting skin care in post-acute settings.

Post-Acute Leaders & Operators

SNF, long-term care, home health, hospice, and rehabilitation leadership.

Industry, Researchers & Students

Industry reps, clinical researchers, and students advancing wound care science.
