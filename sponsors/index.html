Why PAWSIC

A Partnership That Drives Impact

PAWSIC sponsorship delivers more than visibility. It connects your brand with an engaged community of healthcare decision-makers while supporting education that directly improves patient outcomes.

01

Direct Access to Decision-Makers

Your brand reaches wound care clinicians, facility directors, and administrators who influence purchasing decisions across skilled nursing, home health, and long-term care organizations.

02

Year-Round Brand Visibility

Recognition across PAWSIC's website, social media channels, email newsletters, webinar series, and educational events, giving your organization continuous exposure throughout the year.

03

CE/CME Education Partnership

Associate your brand with accredited continuing education, positioning your organization as a supporter of clinical excellence and professional development in wound care.

04

Collaborative Engagement

Opportunities to co-brand, participate in expert panels, sponsor educational webinars, and engage directly with a multidisciplinary wound care audience at live events.

05

Industry Advisory Participation

Higher-tier sponsors gain seats at post-acute round tables and Special Interest Groups (SIGs), contributing to discussions that shape wound care standards and best practices.

06

Mission-Aligned Investment

Every dollar supports evidence-based wound care education, advocacy for elevated standards in post-acute settings, and a growing community of dedicated clinicians nationwide.

Partnering with PAWSIC has given our organization direct access to thousands of wound care professionals who are passionate about improving patient outcomes. The educational events and community engagement have been invaluable for building meaningful relationships in the post-acute space.
