FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in Free membership?

Free members get access to PAWSIC Community Forums, the monthly newsletter, the Basic Resource Library, event announcements and updates, and the Frank & Lizzie Show. No credit card required. It's free forever.

Can I upgrade from Free to Premium anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade to Premium at any time from your account dashboard. Your 30-day free trial starts the day you upgrade, so you can explore all premium benefits before committing.

Is there a credit card required for Free membership?

No. Free membership is completely free: no credit card, no payment information, no catch. You only provide payment details if and when you choose to upgrade to Premium.

Can I cancel Premium anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your Premium membership at any time from your account settings. If you cancel during the 30-day trial, you will not be charged. If you cancel after the trial, you will retain Premium access through the end of your current billing period.

How many CE courses will be available?

PAWSIC is actively developing a growing library of CE/CME accredited courses. Our first course, Person-Centered Wound Care, is available now. Additional courses on pressure injury assessment, incontinence-associated dermatitis, and documentation and coding are in development. All current and future courses are included with Premium membership.

What's the difference between SIG Spaces and Forums?

Forums are open community discussion boards available to all members (free and premium) for general wound care conversation, questions, and peer support. SIG Spaces (Shared Interest Groups) are premium-only, focused workgroups organized around specific clinical topics or initiatives, such as pressure injury prevention, skin failure, or wound care in home health. SIG Spaces provide a more structured, collaborative environment for deeper engagement.
