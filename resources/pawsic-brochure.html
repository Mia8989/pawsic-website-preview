Uniting Around Better Wound Care & Skin Health

PAWSIC is the council for everyone who cares about wound care and skin health. Clinicians, caregivers, patients, families, industry partners, and students are all welcome. We provide practical education, CE & CME credits, and a professional community to improve outcomes everywhere.

What does "post-acute" mean? It simply means care that happens after a hospital stay, including nursing homes, rehab centers, home health, hospice, and assisted living. Millions of patients receive wound care in these settings every year.

Why This Matters

When someone has a chronic wound, they often receive care in many settings, which can become confusing and disconnected, and wound outcomes suffer. PAWSIC was created to change that.

  • Bring together everyone involved in wound care and skin health
  • Provide practical, evidence-based education for real-world care settings
  • Advocate to improve standards and communication across settings and care teams
