Member Spotlight

Meet Alec McCall & William Ash

Co-founders, Red Maple LifeSciences

Alec McCall, Ph.D.

Chief Executive Officer

William J. Ash III, MS

Co-Founder

Alec and William are the co-founders of Red Maple LifeSciences, a startup out of Brown University focused on developing wound care technologies for infection management and accelerated healing. Alec recently completed his PhD in Biomedical Engineering at Brown, where his research focused on wound infections, biomaterials, and smart therapeutic systems. William brings experience in medical device product development, commercialization strategy, and regulatory pathways in the medtech industry.

They were recently accepted into the National Science Foundation (NSF) I-Corps program, where they are conducting customer discovery interviews with clinicians, researchers, and industry leaders across wound care and infectious disease, with support from PAWSIC co-founder Julie Rhodovi.

Why PAWSIC?

"PAWSIC provides an incredible opportunity to connect with experts in the field, gain valuable insights into real-world clinical challenges, and build relationships that can help accelerate the translation of our technology into meaningful patient impact."

Through the NSF I-Corps program, these conversations are especially valuable as we work to ensure our solutions align with the needs of clinicians, patients, and the broader wound care ecosystem.

Alec and William are still conducting NSF I-Corps interviews. PAWSIC members can book a 30-minute conversation directly.
