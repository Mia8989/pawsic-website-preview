Common Questions

Questions You Might Have

Do I need to be a nurse or doctor to join?

Not at all. PAWSIC welcomes everyone: patients, family members, caregivers, and anyone who cares about wound care and skin health. Our free membership is designed with you in mind.

Is it really free?

Yes. Free membership costs nothing and never will. No credit card, no hidden fees. You get access to wound care basics, the newsletter, the Frank & Lizzie Show, community forums, and more.

What kind of wound care information will I find?

You will find easy-to-read guides about pressure injuries, chronic wounds, skin failure, and general wound healing. We also have checklists you can bring to doctor visits and tools to help you track healing progress.

Can I talk to other patients and caregivers?

Yes. Our community forums let you connect with others going through similar experiences. You can ask questions, share what has worked for you, and find support from people who understand.

Is the medical information trustworthy?

All PAWSIC resources are created or reviewed by board-certified wound care professionals. Our content is evidence-based and follows clinical guidelines. That said, it is educational and not a replacement for advice from your own healthcare team.
