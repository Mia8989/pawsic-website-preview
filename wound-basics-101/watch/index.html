Stream the full course on-demand at your own pace. Your free PAWSIC membership is active and a welcome email is on its way with your login details.

3 hours · 4 modules | Watch in full to earn your Certificate of Completion
Course Overview

What's Covered

Four expert-led modules designed for nurses and clinicians working in post-acute wound care settings.

1

Evaluating a Wound: Key Observations

Jennifer Bierhup, RN, CWS · THP

  • Tissue types, wound edges, skin integrity, and drainage
  • Identifying wound characteristics accurately
2

Pressure Injuries vs. Non-PIs

Donna Boyer, RN, CWS · GHC

  • Differentiating pressure injuries from other wound types
  • Staging PIs using NPIAP and MDS (Section M)
  • Management aligned with F686 regulations
3

Treatments 101: Foundational Approaches

Mary Martin, RN, CWS, THP

  • Basic treatment principles and interventions
  • Selecting appropriate wound care products
  • Addressing wound healing challenges
4

Patient-Centered Care and the 4M Framework

Jeanine Maguire, PhD, MPT, CWS · President, PAWSIC

  • Patient-centered care in wound management
  • Mobility, Medications, Mentation, and What Matters
  • Holistic approaches to skin and wound care
