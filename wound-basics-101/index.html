Course Modules

What You'll Learn

Four expert-led modules delivering essential wound care knowledge in an accessible format for LPNs, CNAs, RNs, therapists, and all post-acute care professionals.

01
Module 01

Evaluating a Wound: Key Observations

  • Understanding tissue types, wound edges, skin integrity, and drainage
  • Identifying wound characteristics without clinical assessment terminology
Presented by Jennifer Bierhup, RN, WCC, CCM, IRNPA, FACCWS
02
Module 02

Pressure Injuries vs. Non-PIs

  • Differentiating pressure injuries from other wound types
  • Staging PIs using NPIAP and MDS (Section M)
  • Management strategies aligned with F686 regulations
Presented by Donna Boyer, RN, CWS, QCP-CT
03
Module 03

Treatments 101: Foundational Approaches

  • Basic treatment principles and interventions
  • Selecting appropriate wound care products
  • Addressing wound healing challenges
Presented by Mary Martin, BSN, RN, CWS
04
Module 04

Patient-Centered Care & the 4M Framework

  • Defining patient-centered care in wound management
  • Applying the 4M Framework: Mobility, Medications, Mentation, and What Matters
  • Integrating holistic approaches to skin and wound care
Presented by Jeanine Maguire, PhD, MPT, CWS, FCPP
