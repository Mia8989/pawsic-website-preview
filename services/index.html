Additional Offerings

Beyond our core services, PAWSIC provides:

CE/CME Courses

Continuing education opportunities for nurses, physicians, and other healthcare professionals. Premium members enjoy full access to all available courses.

Community Forums & SIG Spaces

Connect with peers, share best practices, ask questions, and collaborate on challenges facing your organization and specialty area.

Professional Advocacy

PAWSIC advocates for quality standards, policy changes, and professional recognition in the post-acute wound care space.

Post-Acute Wound & Skin Integrity Council — a 501(c)(6) nonprofit uniting wound care professionals through education, community, and advocacy.
