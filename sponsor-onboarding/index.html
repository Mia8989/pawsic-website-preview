Select a level below and we will pre-fill it on the form for you. You can change it anytime on the form itself.

Platinum
$25,000 / year
  • Logo & link on website + social media
  • Exclusive access to education library
  • Gift 25 PAWSIC memberships
  • Monthly Executive Committee updates
  • Voting in quarterly All Members meetings
  • Involvement in Shared Interest Groups

  • Seat at key skin & wound round tables
Gold
$15,000 / year
  • Logo & link on website + social media
  • Exclusive access to education library
  • Gift 10 PAWSIC memberships
  • Monthly Executive Committee updates

  • Acknowledgment at 2 annual events
Silver
$10,000 / year
  • Logo & link on website + social media
  • Exclusive access to education library
  • Gift 5 PAWSIC memberships
  • Monthly Executive Committee updates
  • Voting in quarterly All Members meetings
Bronze
$5,000 / year
  • Logo & link on PAWSIC website
  • Exclusive access to education library
  • Gift 2 PAWSIC memberships
  • Monthly Executive Committee updates
  • Voting in quarterly All Members meetings

$500 to $4,999 / year
  • Logo on PAWSIC website
  • Access to education library
