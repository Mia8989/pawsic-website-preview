Episode 01

32 Years Without a Diagnosis: Amy Rivera's Lymphedema

Amy Rivera waited 32 years before her lymphedema was named. In our launch episode she shares her decades-long path to a diagnosis with PAWSIC clinicians Jennifer Bierhup and Therese Laub, and what every home health and post-acute clinician can learn from a story that should not have taken that long.

Your Hosts

Clinicians on a mission to educate

Jennifer and Therese host Healing by Design because the wound care education most clinicians need is rarely the wound care education they get. Every episode is built around that gap.

Jennifer Bierhup

RN, WCC, CCM, iRNPA, FACCWS

Wound care nurse, educator, and PAWSIC clinical leader. Brings decades of bedside and home health perspective to every conversation.

Therese Laub

PAWSIC Clinical Educator

Clinical educator and co-host. Focused on translating evidence-based wound care into the realities of post-acute and home health practice.
