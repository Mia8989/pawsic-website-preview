Why PAWSIC

What Makes PAWSIC Different

Many wound care organizations focus on acute or hospital-based care. PAWSIC is built specifically for the post-acute world, where wound and skin care challenges are unique and often underserved.

Post-acute focused: Built for SNFs, home health, hospice, and long-term care
Interprofessional by design: Welcoming every role on the care team
Evidence-based education: Accredited CE/CME courses led by clinical experts
Free to join: Start with free membership, upgrade when you see the value
