Medical Advice Disclaimer

Important Disclaimer

Responses in this forum reflect individual clinical knowledge and peer expertise. They are not PAWSIC-endorsed clinical recommendations, practice guidelines, or medical advice. No provider-patient relationship is created through this forum.

  • All clinical decisions must be made by the treating provider based on individual patient assessment
  • PAWSIC, its moderators, and responding clinicians assume no liability for patient outcomes resulting from information shared in this forum

Asking Questions

  • Frame clinical questions clearly with relevant (de-identified) context: wound type, care setting, patient population, current interventions
  • Never include real patient identifiers, facility names, or any information that could identify a specific patient. Use composite or hypothetical scenarios
  • Specify whether you are asking about general best practice, a specific clinical scenario, or seeking resource recommendations
Well-Framed Question Example

"In a skilled nursing setting, what does current evidence support for repositioning frequency in patients on high-density foam mattresses who are at end of life with limited mobility? Looking for guideline-based recommendations."

Answering Questions

  • Ground your responses in current evidence-based practice. Cite sources when possible (guideline body, journal, year)
  • Clearly distinguish between evidence-based recommendations, institutional protocols, and personal clinical experience
  • Use appropriate clinical terminology. Preferred: "pressure injury" (not "bedsore"), "wound closure" (not "healing guarantee")
  • Use hedging language: "current evidence supports," "guidelines recommend," "clinical experience suggests" -- never absolute outcome claims
  • If you lack expertise in the specific area, defer rather than speculate

HIPAA Compliance (Mandatory)

Zero Tolerance

Violation of patient privacy will result in immediate removal and potential suspension.

  • All case-based questions and answers must be fully de-identified per the HIPAA Safe Harbor method
  • No real patient photos unless fully de-identified with documented consent (which cannot be verified through this forum -- avoid posting patient images entirely)

Conflicts of Interest

  • If your response references a product or device with which you have a financial relationship, disclose it
  • Do not use Q&A responses as an opportunity for product promotion
  • Provide balanced perspectives -- when mentioning a specific product, note the broader category or alternatives
Disclosure Required

Transparency about financial relationships ensures the integrity of clinical peer exchange. If in doubt, disclose.

Scope and Limitations

  • This forum does not replace formal clinical education, CE/CME programs, or institutional clinical protocols
  • Questions requiring urgent clinical decision-making should be directed to the treating clinical team, not this forum
  • PAWSIC reserves the right to add editorial disclaimers to responses that may be incomplete or require additional context

Content Moderation

  • Moderators with clinical expertise may tag responses as "peer perspective," "evidence-supported," or "needs citation"
  • Unsubstantiated claims or responses that could lead to patient harm will be removed
  • Repeated guideline violations may result in suspension
