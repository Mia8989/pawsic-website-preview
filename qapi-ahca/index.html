Program Overview

A roadmap for clinical and operational excellence

Move beyond traditional education to a rigorous roadmap for clinical and operational excellence. Combining expert insights from a national multidisciplinary panel with proprietary tools (PAWSIC Center Self-Assessment, Wound Rounds Playbook, Monthly Skin/Wound Audit Guides), we empower your team to pinpoint systemic gaps and implement measurable, sustainable process improvements using a QAPI approach across post-acute settings (SNF, LTC, and assisted living), across team members, across shifts, and over time.

What's In It For Your Centers

  • On-demand virtual access, designed for 24/7 facility schedules
  • 2 CE credits for the designated Skin Health/QAPI Lead
  • Certificates of Completion for all participating clinical staff
  • PAWSIC QAPI Toolkit: Center Self-Assessment, Wound Rounds Playbook, Monthly Audit Tool
  • 30-minute live debrief Q&A with a PAWSIC wound specialist
  • Build a customized Process Improvement Plan (PIP) with F-tag aligned, measurable goals

Format

  • 60-day enrollment window per facility
  • 2+ hours of Lead Clinician content (Assessment to Action, F-tag aligned)
  • 4+ hours of Wound Basics modules for the full clinical team (CNAs, LPNs, RNs, PTs)
  • On-demand, self-paced delivery
  • Center-level PAWSIC Certificate of Completion upon full team completion
