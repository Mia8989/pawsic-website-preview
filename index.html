Why PAWSIC

Why PAWSIC

When someone has a chronic wound, they often receive care in many settings, which can become confusing and disconnected, and wound outcomes suffer.

PAWSIC was created to change that.

Bring together everyone involved in skin health and wound care
Provide practical, evidence-based education for real-world care settings
Advocate to improve standards and communication across settings and care teams

We are a 501(c)(6) nonprofit council led by nationally recognized wound care experts.

Our Mission

We are a patient-centered, interprofessional team supporting the post-acute community with clear guidance on effective wound healing, wound prevention, and skin-injury prevention.

We do this through collaborative evidence-based education, practice, and advocacy.

Become a Member

Our Vision

We believe quality skin and wound care should be available to everyone. That means shifting away from the institutional-centric, wound-focused approach toward a patient-centric framework that addresses the complexity of the whole person, and what matters most to them.

Our committees and shared interest groups lead the initiatives that drive change across the post-acute continuum.
